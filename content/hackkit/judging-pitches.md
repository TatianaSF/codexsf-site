### Pitch shape

- Problem: what changed for the user?
- Demo: what works now?
- Workflow: where Codex or AI helped.
- Next step: what would improve with more time?

### Judging lens

Use transparent criteria: usefulness, clarity, working demo, learning value, and responsible execution.
