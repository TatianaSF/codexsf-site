### Suggested team roles

- Driver: keeps the prototype moving.
- Navigator: tracks the plan and next decision.
- Demo lead: prepares the final story.
- Research lead: validates user need and examples.

### Guidance

Roles should help teams communicate. They should not become private assignments or attendance records in this public site.
