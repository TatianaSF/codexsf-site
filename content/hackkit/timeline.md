### Suggested public timeline

- Four weeks out: publish theme, format, and participation path.
- Two weeks out: share prep materials and accessibility notes.
- One week out: confirm public agenda and demo expectations.
- Event day: orient, build, checkpoint, demo, recap.
- After event: publish public recap and reusable lessons.

### Keep private

Budgets, vendor coordination, staff assignments, and partner discussions should stay outside the public repository.
