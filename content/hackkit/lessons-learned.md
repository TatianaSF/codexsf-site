### Capture publicly useful lessons

- What made participants build faster?
- Which instructions reduced confusion?
- Which format choices improved demos?
- What would another organizer reuse?

### Avoid

Do not publish private critiques, personal participant details, partner notes, budgets, or internal retrospectives.
