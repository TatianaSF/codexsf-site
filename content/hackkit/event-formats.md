### Good public formats

- Mini-hack: two to three hours, one focused prompt, lightweight demos.
- Sprint day: a longer build session with mentor checkpoints.
- Showcase night: short demos, peer feedback, and open discussion.
- Workshop plus build: guided workflow first, then hands-on shipping.

### Selection rule

Choose the smallest format that lets participants leave with a working artifact or a clearer next step.
