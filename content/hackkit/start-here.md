### Purpose

HackKit helps organizers create practical, welcoming hackathons where builders can move from idea to demo without needing a private planning archive.

### Public starter frame

- Pick one clear builder outcome.
- Keep the format small enough to explain in one minute.
- Publish only participant-facing expectations.
- Keep private operations outside this public repository.
